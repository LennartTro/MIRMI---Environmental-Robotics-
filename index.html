Our Research

We develop robotic systems and AI methods for environmental monitoring, sustainability, and societally relevant applications.

Our Vision

We envision a future where autonomous robotic systems seamlessly interact with natural environments — across air, land and water — to support sustainable environmental monitoring and intervention.

Our Mission

We develop robust, autonomous robotic platforms that operate across environmental domains — air, ground and water — to enable meaningful interaction with and understanding ecosystems. We work at the intersection of robotics, perception and environmental science to close the gap between engineering innovation and ecological impact.
